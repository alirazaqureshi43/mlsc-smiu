Events Conducted

Info Desk

The information desk was abuzz with activity as enthusiastic students worked diligently to raise awareness about the MLSC The dedicated members of MLSC had taken it upon themselves to ensure that every student was aware of the club's presence and the opportunities it provided. At the information desk, vibrant banners proudly displayed MLSC's logo, instantly capturing the attention of passersby. The information desk proved to be an invaluable tool in spreading the word about MLSC's impact on campus

MLSC Launch

The day had finally arrived for the much-anticipated launch event of the MICROSOFT LEARN STUDENTS CLUB (MLSC). Excitement filled the air as students and faculty gathered in the university's QA Hall, eager to witness the unveiling of this groundbreaking initiative. The launch event had successfully achieved its objective of not only introducing MLSC to the university but also igniting a spark of curiosity and ambition among attendees. The stage was now set for MLSC to embark on its journey of fostering innovation, collaboration, and learning.

Azure Workshop

Sanjana Kirshan, the esteemed lead of the MICROSOFT LEARN STUDENTS CLUB (MLSC), organized an illuminating workshop on Azure, the renowned cloud computing platform. The workshop was a testament to Sanjana's expertise and dedication in equipping students with practical skills in the field of machine learning and data science.Sanjana's workshop was thoughtfully structured, starting with an introduction to Azure's fundamental concepts and gradually progressing to more advanced topics.

Python Workshop

Saram Hai, a talented and enthusiastic university student, took the initiative to organize an enlightening Python workshop for fellow students. With a deep passion for coding and a desire to share knowledge, Saram embarked on this endeavor to empower others with the essential programming skills offered by Python. The workshop began with Saram's infectious energy as they introduced the participants to the fundamentals of Python programming. With clarity and patience, Saram explained the syntax, data types, control structures, and built-in functions of Python, ensuring that everyone had a solid foundation to build upon.

Microsoft EDU Day

MLSC-SMIU played a pivotal role as one of the key organizers of Microsoft EDU Day 2023, held at the prestigious Marriott Hotel Karachi. This highly anticipated event brought together students, educators, and professionals from the region to explore the latest advancements in technology and education.

AI-900 Study Group

Sanjana formed an AI-900 study group to help students prepare for the Microsoft AI-900 certification exam. The group collaborated on study sessions, shared resources, and discussed concepts. To motivate participation, Sanjana offered a LinkedIn Premium membership to the winner and provided a Microsoft exam voucher for passing the AI-900 exam with a high score.

Online Sessions

We organized several online sessions, including a Microsoft Summit watch party, an Introduction to MLSA (Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador), a session on Chatgenie (chatbot development platform), and a session on the fundamentals of Data Engineering. These sessions aimed to provide valuable insights and practical knowledge to participants, fostering collaboration and skill development within the MLSC-SMIU
